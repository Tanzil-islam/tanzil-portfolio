I am Kazi Md Tanzil Islam, an AI researcher and engineer passionate about pushing the boundaries of what machines can learn and reason about. My work spans three core domains: deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

"I believe the most powerful AI is not the most complex — it is the most purposefully designed."

From fine-tuning large language models to building end-to-end vision pipelines, I approach each problem with a blend of rigorous research intuition and engineering pragmatism. I am particularly interested in multimodal learning — systems that can see, read, and reason simultaneously.

When I am not training models, I write about AI research, mentor aspiring engineers, and contribute to open-source projects.
